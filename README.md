# Power-Bi-Project
1. Project Title
Consumer Electronics Sales Analytics – End-to-End Power BI Capstone Project

2. Project Description / Purpose
This project focuses on building an end-to-end business intelligence solution using Power BI to analyze five years of sales and transaction data for a consumer electronics company operating across the United States.
The purpose of the project is to transform raw, multi-file data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making for management, sales teams, and stakeholders.

3. Technologies Used
   1.   Power BI Desktop
   2.   Power Query (ETL)
   3.   DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
   4.   Power BI Service
   5.   AI Visuals (Key Influencers, Smart Narrative)
   6.   Excel (Data Source)

4. Data Sources
   The project uses multiple Excel datasets:
   1.  Customer Profile Data – customer demographics and location details
   2.  Product Data – product names, categories, and pricing
   3.  Sales Data – transactional sales records across multiple files
   4.  Salesperson Data – employee details and assignment information
   5.  Transaction Data – order dates, year, quarter, and month information
  
5. Key Features & Highlights
  1.   Business Problem
       The company lacked a centralized reporting system to track sales performance, employee productivity, and product profitability across years, regions, and categories. Decision-
       makers needed a consolidated, interactive, and shareable dashboard to identify trends, top performers, and improvement areas.
  2.   Project Goals
       1. Analyze yearly and quarterly revenue trends
       2. Evaluate customer growth and geographic reach
       3. Identify top and bottom performing products, categories, employees, and states
       4. Measure employee performance based on sales quantity and revenue
       5. Enable AI-driven insights and self-service analytics
       6. Deliver a report optimized for web, mobile, and PDF sharing  
  3.   Walkthrough of Key Visuals
       1.  KPI Cards displaying total revenue, customers, employees, products, categories, and states served
       2.  Yearly Revenue & Customer Trends using tabular and card visuals
       3.  Employee Performance Dashboard with slicers for year, quarter, and employee ID
       4.  Top & Bottom 5 Analysis for products, product categories, employees, and states
       5.  Quarterly Revenue Comparison to identify seasonal sales patterns
       6.  Key Influencers Visual to highlight products driving revenue growth
       7.  Smart Narrative provides an automated executive summary
  4.   Business Impact
       1.  Improved visibility into sales and profitability trends across multiple years
       2.  Enabled management to identify high-performing products and underperforming regions
       3.  Provided actionable insights into employee productivity and sales contribution
       4.  Reduced manual reporting efforts through automated dashboards
       5.  Delivered a scalable, shareable, and mobile-friendly analytics solution for stakeholders
